Structure:
* Products belong to categories → subcategories → have properties.

* Vendors (hidden from customers) stock products with inventory levels.

* Customers only see products & availability, not vendors.

* Orders link customers, products, and delivery agents.

________________


Kuzzi Product & Order Management Schema (ERD)
Entities & Relationships
________________


1. Users
   * UserID (PK)

   * Name

   * PhoneNumber

   * Email

   * UserType (Customer, Vendor, DeliveryAgent, Support)

   * Location

________________


2. Categories
      * CategoryID (PK)

      * CategoryName (e.g., Electronics, Fashion, Groceries)

________________


3. Subcategories
         * SubcategoryID (PK)

         * SubcategoryName (e.g., Smartphones, Laptops, Footwear)

         * CategoryID (FK → Categories.CategoryID)

________________


4. Products
            * ProductID (PK)

            * ProductName

            * Description

            * SubcategoryID (FK → Subcategories.SubcategoryID)

________________


5. ProductProperties (dynamic attributes depending on category)
               * PropertyID (PK)

               * PropertyName (e.g., Size, Color, BatteryLife, Warranty)

               * SubcategoryID (FK → Subcategories.SubcategoryID)

________________


6. ProductPropertyValues
                  * ValueID (PK)

                  * ProductID (FK → Products.ProductID)

                  * PropertyID (FK → ProductProperties.PropertyID)

                  * Value (e.g., “Red”, “128GB”, “2 years”)

________________


7. VendorInventory
                     * InventoryID (PK)

                     * VendorID (FK → Users.UserID, UserType=Vendor)

                     * ProductID (FK → Products.ProductID)

                     * QuantityAvailable

                     * LastConfirmed (timestamp of vendor ping/check-in)

________________


8. Orders
                        * OrderID (PK)

                        * CustomerID (FK → Users.UserID, UserType=Customer)

                        * OrderStatus (Pending, Confirmed, Dispatched, Delivered, Cancelled)

                        * CreatedAt

                        * UpdatedAt

________________


9. OrderItems
                           * OrderItemID (PK)

                           * OrderID (FK → Orders.OrderID)

                           * ProductID (FK → Products.ProductID)

                           * QuantityOrdered

                           * Price

________________


10. Deliveries
                              * DeliveryID (PK)

                              * OrderID (FK → Orders.OrderID)

                              * DeliveryAgentID (FK → Users.UserID, UserType=DeliveryAgent)

                              * Status (Assigned, PickedUp, InTransit, Delivered, Cancelled)

                              * AssignedAt

                              * DeliveredAt

________________


11. SupportTickets
                                 * TicketID (PK)

                                 * OrderID (FK → Orders.OrderID)

                                 * CustomerID (FK → Users.UserID)

                                 * IssueType (ProductNotAvailable, DamagedItem, LateDelivery, Cancellation)

                                 * ResolutionStatus (Open, In Progress, Resolved, Escalated)

                                 * CreatedAt

                                 * ResolvedAt

________________


Relationships Summary
                                    * Users → Vendors, Customers, Delivery Agents, Support

                                    * Categories → Subcategories → Products

                                    * Products → have ProductProperties (dynamic)

                                    * Vendors → maintain stock in VendorInventory

                                    * Customers → place Orders → containing OrderItems

                                    * Orders → assigned to Deliveries (handled by Delivery Agents)

                                    * SupportTickets → resolve issues with Orders

________________


📌 How this helps Casua:
                                       * Products are categorized in a structured way with flexible properties.

                                       * Vendor stock is tracked without being visible to customers.

                                       * Orders seamlessly connect customers, inventory, and delivery.

                                       * Support workflows are embedded into the system.
